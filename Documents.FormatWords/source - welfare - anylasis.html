• 1 the general health, happiness and safety of a person, an animal or a group synonym well-being We are concerned about the child's welfare.
  • 2 practical or financial help that is provided, often by the government, for people or animals that need it The state is still the main provider of welfare. child welfare a social welfare programme welfare provision/services/work See related entries: Helping others
  • 3 ( especially North American English ) ( British English also social security ) money that the government pays regularly to people who are poor, unemployed, sick, etc. They would rather work than live on welfare. See related entries: Helping others
